WanderIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

The short version

WanderIQ does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Your trip data belongs to you.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter in WanderIQ — trips, checklists, itineraries, packing lists, places, and reminders — is stored on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, the app also syncs this data to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit, so it appears on your other devices and can be shared with family members you invite. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy; the developer has no access to it.

Family sharing

When you share a trip, Apple's iCloud sharing delivers it only to the people you explicitly invite. You can stop sharing at any time from within the app or your iCloud settings.

Place search and maps

When you search for a place or open a map, your search text and approximate map region are sent to Apple Maps to return results, under Apple's privacy terms. WanderIQ does not request access to your device location.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data leaves your phone.

Children

WanderIQ collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date.

WanderIQ 隐私政策

最近更新：2026 年 6 月 12 日

简要说明

WanderIQ 不收集、不存储、也不分享您的任何个人数据。没有账号系统、 没有数据分析、没有广告、也没有任何第三方 SDK。您的旅行数据完全属于您自己。

数据存储位置

您在 WanderIQ 中输入的所有内容——行程、清单、日程、行李打包列表、 地点和提醒——都存储在您自己的设备上。如果您登录了 iCloud， 应用会通过苹果 CloudKit 将数据同步到您个人的 iCloud 账户， 以便在您的其他设备上使用，并可与您邀请的家庭成员共享。这些数据受 苹果隐私政策约束， 开发者无法访问。

家庭共享

共享行程时，苹果 iCloud 共享只会将数据发送给您明确邀请的人。 您可以随时在应用内或 iCloud 设置中停止共享。

地点搜索与地图

当您搜索地点或打开地图时，搜索文字和大致地图区域会发送给 苹果地图以返回结果，受苹果隐私条款约束。 WanderIQ 不请求获取您的设备位置。

通知

提醒在您的设备本地安排，通知数据不会离开您的手机。

儿童

WanderIQ 不收集任何人的数据，包括儿童。

政策变更

如本政策有任何变更，更新版本将发布在本页面并注明新日期。
