```
WanderIQ is the simple, family-friendly trip planner: every booking,
every day of the itinerary, and every packing item in one checklist app
that the whole family shares.

PLAN THE TRIP
• Create trips with dates and destinations
• Prep list for time-sensitive bookings, hotels, and documents
• Day-by-day itinerary — today is highlighted automatically while traveling
• Attach a time and owner to any item so nothing falls on one person

NEVER MISS A BEAT
• Add a reminder to any item — ticket sales, check-ins, departures —
  and get a notification at exactly the right moment
• Trip countdown and per-day progress at a glance

FIND YOUR WAY
• Attach real places to itinerary items with built-in place search
• Open any place in Apple Maps with one swipe
• See each day's stops together on a pin map

SHARE WITH FAMILY
• Share a trip via iCloud — everyone sees the same live checklist
• Checkmarks and edits sync to all participants automatically
• Works offline; changes sync when you're back online

PACK SMART
• Daily go-out packing checklist
• One-tap reset for the next day

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no ads, no analytics
• Your data stays on your devices and in your own iCloud
• Fully bilingual: English and Simplified Chinese

Built by a parent, for family trips. Have a great one!
```
