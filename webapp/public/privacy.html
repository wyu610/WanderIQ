WanderIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

The short version

WanderIQ uses an account so your trips can sync across your devices and be shared with people you invite. We collect only your email address and the trip content you create, store it securely with our backend provider, and use it solely to run the app. There is no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and we never sell your data. You can delete your trips — or your entire account and all its data — at any time.

What we collect

  • Email address — to create and sign in to your account.
  • Your trip content — the trips, checklists, itineraries, packing lists, places, notes, and reminder times you enter.
  • An account identifier — a random ID that links your data to your account.

We do not collect your device location, contacts, photos, analytics, or any advertising identifiers.

Where your data lives

Your account and trip data are stored using Supabase, our backend provider, on secured cloud servers. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and protected by per-account access rules so that only you — and the people you choose to share a trip with — can read or change it.

How we use it

We use your data only to: sign you in, sync your trips across your devices and the web app, and deliver trips to the people you invite. We do not use it for advertising or analytics, and we do not share or sell it to third parties.

Sharing a trip

When you invite someone to a trip by email, that trip becomes accessible to that person (as a viewer or editor, as you choose) once they sign in to WanderIQ with the same email. Only the trips you explicitly share are visible to them. You control sharing from within the app.

Maps

When you open a place on a map, the place name or search text is sent to Apple Maps or Google Maps to show the location, under those companies' privacy terms. WanderIQ does not request your device location.

Notifications

Trip reminders are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data leaves your phone.

Deleting your data

You can delete any individual trip from within the app. To delete your entire account together with all of your trips and personal data, use Delete Account in the app, or email us at the address below and we will permanently remove your account and data.

Children

WanderIQ is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests? Email wyu610@gmail.com.

WanderIQ 隐私政策

最近更新：2026 年 6 月 15 日

简要说明

WanderIQ 使用账号，以便您的行程能在多台设备间同步，并与您邀请的人共享。 我们仅收集您的电子邮箱地址和您创建的行程内容，通过后端服务商安全存储， 并仅用于运行本应用。没有广告、没有数据分析、没有追踪，我们也绝不出售您的数据。 您可以随时删除行程，或删除整个账号及其全部数据。

我们收集的内容

  • 电子邮箱地址——用于创建和登录您的账号。
  • 您的行程内容——您输入的行程、清单、日程、行李打包列表、 地点、备注和提醒时间。
  • 账号标识符——将您的数据与账号关联的随机 ID。

我们不收集您的设备位置、通讯录、照片、分析数据或任何广告标识符。

数据存储位置

您的账号和行程数据通过我们的后端服务商 Supabase 存储在受保护的云服务器上。 数据在传输过程中加密（HTTPS），并受按账号划分的访问规则保护， 只有您本人——以及您选择共享行程的人——才能读取或修改。

我们如何使用

我们仅将您的数据用于：登录、在您的多台设备和网页应用之间同步行程、 以及把行程发送给您邀请的人。我们不会将其用于广告或分析， 也不会与第三方共享或出售。

共享行程

当您通过电子邮箱邀请他人加入某个行程时，该人以您指定的身份（查看者或编辑者） 在使用同一邮箱登录 WanderIQ 后即可访问该行程。只有您明确共享的行程对其可见。 您可在应用内管理共享。

地图

当您在地图上打开某个地点时，地点名称或搜索文字会发送给 苹果地图或谷歌地图以显示位置，受相应公司的隐私条款约束。 WanderIQ 不请求获取您的设备位置。

通知

行程提醒在您的设备本地安排，通知数据不会离开您的手机。

删除您的数据

您可以在应用内删除任何单个行程。如需删除整个账号及您的全部行程和个人数据， 请使用应用内的删除账号功能，或发送邮件至下方地址， 我们将永久删除您的账号和数据。

儿童

WanderIQ 并非面向儿童，我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的数据。

政策变更

如本政策有任何变更，更新版本将发布在本页面并注明新日期。

联系方式

如有疑问或删除请求，请发邮件至 wyu610@gmail.com。
