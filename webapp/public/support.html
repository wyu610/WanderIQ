Common questions

How do I sync across devices? Sign in with the same account (email, Apple, or Google) on each device — your trips sync automatically.

How do I share a trip? Open a trip, tap the share button, and invite someone by email as a viewer or editor. They'll see it after signing in with that email.

How do I delete my account? Open the account menu and choose Delete Account — this permanently removes your account and all your trips. You can also email us to request deletion.

Is there a web version? Yes — the same account works at wander-iq.vercel.app.

常见问题

如何在多台设备间同步？在每台设备上使用同一账号（邮箱、Apple 或 Google）登录，您的行程会自动同步。

如何共享行程？打开行程，点按共享按钮，通过电子邮箱以查看者或 编辑者身份邀请他人。对方使用该邮箱登录后即可看到。

如何删除账号？打开账号菜单并选择删除账号， 即可永久删除您的账号和所有行程。您也可以发邮件请求删除。

有网页版吗？有——同一账号可在 wander-iq.vercel.app 使用。
